# Poker Closed Beta — Tester Welcome Guide

Thank you for helping test Chợ Cóc FKO Poker! You are in a **Closed Beta** — a private,
pre-release version. This guide gets you started and shows how to give feedback that helps.

---

## 1. What "Closed Beta" means

- **Play-money only.** All chips are virtual "xu". Nothing here is real money.
- **Expect rough edges.** This is a test build. You may hit bugs — that's exactly what we
  want to find.
- **Your data may reset.** Test tables and stacks can be cleared between test rounds.
- **It's private.** Please don't share links, screenshots, or access with people outside the
  Beta.

When you first sit down you'll be asked to **acknowledge the Beta terms** — a one-time
confirmation of the points above. You must accept before you can take a seat.

---

## 2. Getting in

- You'll receive access on the account (email) you were invited with. If poker doesn't appear,
  you're not on the current cohort's allowlist yet — cohorts are opened in stages.
- Every poker screen shows a **BETA** badge and a link bar (Rules · Known issues · Support).

---

## 3. How to play

- Read the **Rules** and try the **Learn/Training** sandbox first if poker is new to you
  (linked from the poker home).
- Join a table from the lobby or create one. 2–6 players, No-Limit Hold'em cash game.
- The **server decides everything** — cards, whose turn, legal actions, pots, winners. If the
  screen looks wrong, refresh; the authoritative state will reconcile.

---

## 4. How to file a good report

Use the **"Report a problem"** button on the table (bottom corner). It automatically attaches
the technical context we need — you don't have to copy anything by hand.

A good report has:

1. **Category** — pick the closest: rules, coin, realtime, reconnect, ui, mobile, performance,
   translation, sound, abuse, or other.
2. **Severity** — blocker (couldn't play) / major / minor / cosmetic.
3. **What happened**, and if you can, **what you expected** vs **what actually happened**.
4. Optionally a **screenshot link**.

The report auto-includes: build version, your device/browser, table ID, hand ID, and the game
state. It **never** includes your cards, other players' cards, the deck, or any credentials.

You can also copy the **table ID** from the beta link bar to quote it to support.

**What to look for especially:**
- Wrong pot, wrong winner, wrong amount to call, or chips that don't add up.
- Getting stuck after a disconnect / reconnect; the table not updating until you refresh.
- Anything confusing about whose turn it is or how much to bet (use "UX feedback").
- Text in the wrong language or untranslated.

---

## 5. Where things are

- **Known issues:** the "Known issues" link in the beta bar (`/games/poker/known-issues`).
- **Rules:** the "Rules" link.
- **Support / anything else:** the "Support" link (`/feedback`).

---

## 6. House rules for testers

- Play fair. Collusion, stalling, and abuse are logged and reviewed; it can get you suspended
  from the Beta.
- Keep it private (see §1).
- Have fun and break things — respectfully. Thank you! 🃏
